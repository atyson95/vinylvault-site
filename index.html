VinylVault

A companion for a serious record collection.

VinylVault is a personal, local-first app for managing a 7,000+ record vinyl collection: instant shelf lookup, DJ set building with tempo- and key-aware sequencing, digital library matching, and playback integration with Roon and Tidal.

It is currently in private development and pilot testing.

Tempo (BPM) data for records without digital audio is provided by GetSongBPM.
